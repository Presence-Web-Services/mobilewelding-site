Metal Repair and Fabrication Services

We offer mobile and portable metal welding, cutting, repair, and fabrication services in Stevens Point and across central Wisconsin.

Contact us today

About Us

Mobile Welding LLC was founded in 2022 in Stevens Point, Wisconsin. Educated from Mid-State Technical College and UW-Madison, we are capable of handling any project big or small. As a member of the American Welding Society (AWS), we abide by ethical standards in our work. We offer a competitive price point, taking pride in always doing the job right the first time. Our services come with a lifetime guarantee, with free follow up service if any welds fail under normal use. We maintain a calm, collected, professional attitude while working with a sense of urgency to get the customer back in operation as quickly as possible.

Welding Capabilities

We are capable of welding mild steel and stainless steel up to 1/2 inch thick, using any process:

  • MIG (GMAW)
  • TIG (GTAW)
  • Stick (SMAW)

Cutting Capabilities

We are capable of cutting all types of metal, up to 1/2 inch thick.
